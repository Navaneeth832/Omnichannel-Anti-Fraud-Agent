💸 Script 4: Fake Google Pay / UPI Refund Scam
Characters:
Scammer (pretending to be Google Pay Support Agent "Amit Verma")
Victim (named Priya)

Scammer: Hello, am I speaking to Ms. Priya Sharma?
Victim: Yes, who is this?
Scammer: Ma'am, I am Amit Verma calling from Google Pay Customer Support, Bangalore. We have detected that a transaction of ₹8,500 was incorrectly debited from your Google Pay account linked to your number ending in 4521 on 27th May. This was a system error from our side and we sincerely apologize for the inconvenience.
Victim: Oh really? I did notice some transaction but wasn't sure—
Scammer: Yes ma'am, that is exactly why we are calling all affected customers personally. We are processing refunds right now and yours will be credited within 10 minutes. I just need to verify your identity first. Can you please open your Google Pay app right now?
Victim: Okay, I have it open.
Scammer: Perfect. Now ma'am I am sending you a refund request of ₹8,500 to your UPI ID. You will see a notification on your screen. Please enter your UPI PIN to confirm receipt of the refund. This PIN is just to authorize the incoming amount into your account.
Victim: Okay I can see a request—
Scammer: Yes ma'am please go ahead and enter your PIN quickly, this refund link expires in 2 minutes. Also please do not close the app or the refund will be cancelled and we will not be able to process it again for 30 days. Once you confirm I will send you an official refund receipt on this number.
(In reality the victim is authorizing an outgoing payment, not receiving a refund)
