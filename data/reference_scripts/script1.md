🎭 Script 1: Digital Arrest Scam Call
Characters:
Scammer (pretending to be CBI Officer "Rajesh Kumar")
Victim (named Priya)

Scammer: Hello, am I speaking to Ms. Priya Sharma?
Victim: Yes, who is this?
Scammer: Ma'am, I am Officer Rajesh Kumar calling from the Cyber Crime Division of the Central Bureau of Investigation, New Delhi. Your Aadhaar number XXXX-XXXX-4521 has been flagged in a major money laundering case involving illegal international transactions worth ₹47 lakhs.
Victim: What? I haven't done anything—
Scammer: Ma'am, DO NOT interrupt. This is an active federal investigation. A digital arrest warrant has already been issued in your name by the Mumbai High Court. You are legally required to remain on this call. If you disconnect, our officers will arrive at your home within 30 minutes.
Victim: Please, I don't understand—
Scammer: Listen carefully. To avoid immediate physical arrest, you must transfer ₹2,50,000 to a government escrow account RIGHT NOW for verification purposes. This amount will be fully refunded after your name is cleared. Do NOT tell your family. This call is being recorded as official evidence. Switch to WhatsApp video immediately so we can verify your identity.
